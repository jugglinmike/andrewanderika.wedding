FIRST DATE to PROPOSAL

At a Boston College football game in the fall of 2008, with a crisp autumnal breeze blowing across the Heights, Erika and Andrew first bumped into each other. The meeting was to be brief, however, as the future couple was interrupted by Andrew’s college roommate and occasional cousin, Nathan Keegan, who instantly knew the couple was meant to be. Ten months later, a more mature Andrew finally asked Erika on a date to the New England Aquarium.

Thanks to Erika’s sensible friend Annie Muscarella and few outstanding Illinois moving violations, she said yes to the date. The relationship prospered from there, as true and distinctive as the bark of the captive New England harbor seal, and after a summer back home Erika decided to return to Boston for good. Or so Andrew thought.

After four years of adventuring around the greater Boston area, Chicago came calling for Erika. And although Andrew dearly loved living in his parents’ basement, he longed for a reunion in the Windy City. So, a short three years later, Andrew and Erika are reuniting in Chicago, happy to finally be back together again.

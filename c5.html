From the NE Aquarium to Amsterdam

Andrew and Erika met in the fall of 2008 thanks to the sage wisdom of Andrew's college roommate, Nathan Keegan, who instantly knew the couple was meant to be. Andrew took his time, realizing he had some maturing to do, and finally asked Erika on a date to the New England aquarium nine months later.

Thanks to Erika’s sensible friend Annie Muscarella, she said yes to the date, despite some early hesitation. The relationship blossomed from here, and after a summer back home Erika decided to return to Boston for good. Or so Andrew thought.

After three years of dating and living in Boston, Erika whimsically decided to move to Chicago, leaving Andrew to live his parent’s basement. A short three years later, Andrew and Erika are reuniting in Chicago, happy to finally be back together again.
